# No Sweat Rank Information Policy

This Rank Information Policy defines the hierarchy, responsibilities, and progression system used within the **No Sweat** clan.

Ranks exist to ensure clear leadership, fair progression, and accountability across all levels of the community.

---

## 1. Rank Categories

No Sweat ranks are divided into four categories:

1. Leadership  
2. Management  
3. Clan Members  
4. Recruitment & Training  

Each category serves a specific purpose within the clan structure.

---

## 2. Leadership Ranks

### Founder
- Highest authority within No Sweat
- Holds final decision-making power
- Oversees the overall vision and direction of the clan

This rank is permanent.

---

### Co-Founder
- Assists the Founder in leadership duties
- May act on behalf of the Founder when necessary
- Involved in major decisions and long-term planning

This rank is permanent unless reassigned by leadership.

---

### Ownership
- Trusted leadership role
- Helps manage high-level operations
- Supports both leadership and management teams

Ownership members are selected by Founders.

---

## 3. Management Ranks

### Director of Management
- Oversees all management operations
- Coordinates senior and junior management
- Ensures policies and standards are enforced

---

### Senior Management
- Handles advanced moderation and operations
- Oversees clan events, recruitment, and discipline
- Supports Directors and Ownership

---

### Management
- Day-to-day moderation and organization
- Enforces rules and policies
- Assists members and trainees

---

### Junior Management
- Entry-level staff role
- Assists management with basic tasks
- Learns moderation and leadership responsibilities

Management ranks are appointed by leadership and may be removed if standards are not met.

---

## 4. Clan Member Ranks

### Senior Ranking Clan Member
- Highly trusted and respected member
- Demonstrates leadership qualities
- May mentor lower-ranked members

---

### Veteran Clan Member
- Long-term, loyal member of No Sweat
- Consistent activity and positive behavior
- Acts as a role model within the community

---

### Elite Clan Member
- High-performing and skilled player
- Maintains strong teamwork and discipline
- Granted access to advanced clan resources

---

### Core Clan Member
- Active and reliable member
- Consistently represents No Sweat positively
- Eligible for competitive and organized play

---

### Official Clan Member
- Successfully completed training phase
- Full access to standard clan features
- Expected to remain active and respectful

---

## 5. Recruitment & Training Ranks

### Clan Member in Training
- Trial rank for new members
- Under evaluation by management
- Limited access during training period

Failure to meet expectations may result in removal.

---

### Recruit
- Entry-level rank
- New or pending applicants
- No competitive or advanced privileges

---

## 6. Rank Progression

Rank advancement is based on:

- Activity and participation
- Behavior and conduct
- Skill and performance
- Contribution to the community
- Staff evaluation

Promotions are earned and not requested.

---

## 7. Demotion & Rank Removal

Ranks may be adjusted or removed due to:

- Inactivity
- Policy violations
- Abuse of privileges
- Failure to meet rank expectations

All rank decisions are at staff discretion.

---

## 8. Rank Privileges

- Higher ranks may have access to restricted areas
- Privileges may be revoked if abused
- Rank does not grant immunity from enforcement actions

---

## 9. Policy Updates

This Policy may be updated at any time.  
Continued participation in No Sweat constitutes acceptance of the current version.

---
